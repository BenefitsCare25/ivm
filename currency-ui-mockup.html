How the Currency Conversion section works

  • 1 After AI extracts fields from the PDF invoice, the system scans each amount field for foreign currency symbols (MYR/RM, USD/$, EUR/€, GBP/£, THB/฿, JPY/¥, etc.).
  • 2 The incurred date is taken from portal data (or PDF if unavailable). The MAS historical exchange rate API is queried for that specific date — going back up to 10 days for weekends and public holidays.
  • 3 A Currency Conversion card appears in the Comparison column for each foreign-currency field. It shows: original amount, → SGD equivalent, the exact rate used, and the rate date.
  • 4 The AI comparison sees the raw amounts ("SGD 612.00" vs "MYR 2,000.00") and marks it as Mismatch — the Currency Conversion section explains the discrepancy to the reviewer.
  • 5 Currency conversion is informational only (blue, separate from red FWA alerts). It does not automatically mark the claim as flagged.
